PV Sindhu

P.V. Sindhu is a prominent Indian badminton player known for her exceptional skills and achievements. Born on July 5, 1995, she gained international recognition after winning a silver medal at the 2016 Rio Olympics, becoming the first Indian woman to achieve this milestone. Sindhu has also secured multiple medals at the World Championships, showcasing her talent on the global stage. With her powerful playing style and remarkable determination, she has become a source of inspiration for many young athletes in India, promoting the sport of badminton across the country.
